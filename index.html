faq

How much does it cost?

Every journey begins with a complimentary consultation.

We also offer a 50% discount on your first treatment so you can experience results for yourself.

As with all treatments, the exact cost will vary from client to client. What you pay will depend on the degree of your condition, the type of treatment required, and the number of treatments we recommend. We will discuss this together in our complimentary consultation session to determine the most effective and successful treatment plan for you.

How many treatment sessions do I need?

A course can range from as little as 3 sessions to as many as 8 sessions depending on the treatment plan. Additional follow-up sessions over longer periods may also be necessary in order to maintain the best results. These are generally short sessions (30 minutes to an hour long) so clients can go about the rest of their day uninterrupted. 

What are the side effects of non-invasive aesthetic treatments?

Our body sculpting and wellness treatments utilise non-invasive technology, therefore our clients report little to no side effects. At most, clients will experience temporary redness or warmth around the treated area following their treatment. This is in no way painful and clients can immediately resume their normal activities afterwards. Should you have any concerns, we will be glad to discuss them with you over the phone or during our initial consultation.

Do non-invasive aesthetic treatments actually work?

Our clinic is equipped with the best medically-certified, cutting-edge technology on the market. Our medical grade devices are EMR, TGA, and FDA approved and scientifically proven, backed by several reputable peer-viewed studies that we are happy to provide. For the in-depth science behind each treatment, we recommend checking out our blog where we discuss this in detail.
